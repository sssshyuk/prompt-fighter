## Style Guidelines (All Assets)

- **Art Style:** Photorealistic 3D render / Tekken & Street Fighter 6 inspired
- **Color Palette:** Dark base (#0a0a1a), Neon Blue (#4fa4ff), Neon Red (#ff4f4f), Gold accent (#ffcc00)
- **Mood:** Dark, intense, dramatic, cinematic
- **Quality:** High resolution, minimum 1024x1024
- **Background:** Transparent PNG preferred, solid black acceptable (for compositing with blend modes)
- **Consistency:** All assets should share the same cyberpunk/sci-fi aesthetic
